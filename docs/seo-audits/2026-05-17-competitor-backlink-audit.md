## Executive summary

The Houston band-saw competitive backlink landscape is **bimodal**: a small set of high-trust universal directories that every competitor has claimed (yellowpages.com, superpages.com, dexknows.com, chamberofcommerce.com), and a long tail of PBN/spam domains that every competitor accumulates passively and provides zero defensible signal. **When the spam filter is set to <30 across the three top organic competitors (circlesaw + simase-llc + metalsaw), only three intersection domains survive: yellowpages.com, dexknows.com, superpages.com.** That is the citation floor — pure table stakes, all free to claim, and Saw Service 3G is on **zero** of them. Net-new finding versus the 2026-05-12 audit: `sawservice3g.com` now ranks **organic #3 (absolute #6)** for `band saw repair houston` — the site is live and indexed, but `backlinks_summary` returns empty (DataForSEO has not yet detected a single referring domain pointing to us). The strategic action is concentration, not breadth: the owner can close roughly **60–70% of the realistic citation gap with eight outreach actions**, all of which are free or low-cost, and which sidestep the PBN noise that competitors are drowning in.

## Anchor-text observations

Source: `backlinks_anchors` for metalsaw.com, grovesindustrial.com, circlesaw.com — 30 anchors each.

### metalsaw.com (326 backlinks)
- **Top anchor — exact-match URL:** `"metalsaw.com"` — 150 backlinks across 42 RMDs (46% of total). **Over-optimized.** Google's filter for exact-match-domain spam fires above ~30%.
- **Brand anchor:** `"Metal Sawing Technology"` — 7 backlinks, spam_score 52 (PBN-fed).
- **PBN smear pattern detected** (already noted in 2026-05-12 audit): anchor literally reads `"https://metalsaw.com – You need to Skyrocket your Ahrefs DR, Moz DA, and Majestic TF with real PBN…"`. This is negative-SEO disguised as a citation. Do not replicate; **do** add this domain to a future Search Console Disavow file if a similar attack hits us.

### grovesindustrial.com (2,773 backlinks)
- **Top anchor — image links:** 258 image backlinks with empty anchor (typical for legitimate trade-vendor relationships).
- **Brand anchor:** `"Groves Industrial"` — 13 backlinks across 6 RMDs, spam 5 (clean).
- **Exact-match URL anchor:** `"grovesindustrial.com"` — 171 backlinks across 156 RMDs, spam 48 — clearly being fed by an Indian PBN cluster (TLDs `.in`, `.co.in`, `.net.in`).
- **Healthy signal:** large blocks of SKU-specific anchors like `"MTI 422-332-30"` — these are e-commerce product-feed syndications, very natural pattern. Saw Service 3G can't replicate this (we're service, not product).

### circlesaw.com (1,806 backlinks)
- **Top anchor — image / branded:** 478 image backlinks with no anchor text — supplier and partner imagery.
- **Brand anchor:** `"Circle Saw Builders Supply"` — 36 backlinks across 7 RMDs, spam 40 (mixed).
- **Same PBN smear attack** as metalsaw.com — `"https://circlesaw.com – You need to Skyrocket your Ahrefs DR…"`.

### What to copy / not copy in our outreach pitch wording
- **Copy:** branded short forms — "Saw Service 3G", "Authorized Hyd-Mech Dealer Houston", "Industrial Band Saw Repair Spring TX".
- **Copy:** URL-as-anchor for citations (yellowpages, superpages, etc. will use this naturally).
- **DO NOT copy:** exact-match phrase anchors like "band saw repair houston" or "industrial band saw service houston" stuffed into outreach. Both metalsaw and grovesindustrial show clear over-optimization patterns there.
- **DO NOT copy:** any keyword-stuffed multi-phrase anchor like the 2025-10 pattern seen in metalsaw's profile ("He&m band saw service and replacement parts", "Houston band saw service", "Denver colorado band saw service") — these are obviously SEO-tool-generated.

1. **Hyd-Mech dealer locator** — email the Hyd-Mech rep (the one already issuing the dealership). One-line ask: "Can Saw Service 3G be listed on hydmech.com's authorized dealer locator with our Spring TX address and (281) 704-5589?" Highest-leverage single action in the entire audit.

4. **Chamberofcommerce.com** — free local-biz profile, 15 min.

7. **Processregister.com** — free industrial process listing, 20 min.

Then, the **second wave** (paid or relationship-based):

12. Trade-press pitch (`modernmetals.com`, `productionmachining.com`) — Tier 3, 2–4 hours per pitch, defer to Q3 2026.

**First outreach email to send:** the Hyd-Mech dealer-locator email (action 1). It's the only action where Saw Service 3G has standing **no competitor can replicate** — and it carries the trust signal of an OEM endorsement.
